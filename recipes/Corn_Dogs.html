Homemade Corn Dogs

Love the corn dogs you buy at carnivals but wanted to make them at home so I created this easy batter for frankfurters. Great served with mustard.

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal
  • flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Beef frankfurters

Steps

  1. Make the batter by combining the dry ingredients, then stirring in the milk and egg.
  2. Skewer the frankfurters and roll in the batter.
  3. Fry the corn dogs in hot oil until lightly browned.
